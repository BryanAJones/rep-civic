Design decisions
Auto-generated topics
Topics are pulled from public data sources — school board agendas, APS enrollment reports, budget filings. Each topic card has its own source badge and its own +1 question list. A constituent asking about overcrowding stays in that context rather than a generic box.
General question box
Sits above the topics. Catches anything that doesn't fit a generated topic — and once the candidate claims their profile, general questions migrate to their Q&A tab alongside the topic questions.
Stats on unclaimed profile
Videos and Response rate show em dash (—) not zero. Zero implies the candidate has been active and answered nothing. A dash signals the data simply doesn't exist yet — honest about the state.
Claimed but empty video grid
Three dashed placeholder cells with a + symbol — visible only to constituents on the Videos tab. The empty-state prompt counts the waiting questions, which creates pressure without being accusatory. Response rate stays as — until the first video reply.
Questions persist across states
Questions asked before a candidate joins carry forward into their claimed profile. Nothing is lost. When they post their first video, they can reply to any of them and the reply links back to the original question.
